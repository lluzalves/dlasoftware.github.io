PRIVACY POLICY · DLA SOFTWARE

Last updated: April 7, 2026

DLA Software ("we", "us", "our") operates the mobile game Race XRZ 1000 (the "Game"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the Game.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location. All high-score data is stored locally on your device only using Android's Room database.

2. Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services that may collect data under their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob — used to display interstitial advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve contextual ads. This app is configured as non-child-directed and targets users aged 13 and older. AdMob's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Google Play Billing — used to process in-app purchases (nitro packs, coin packs, Remove Ads). Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store any payment information. Google Play's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

3. Age Requirement

The Game is rated for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from or market to children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the Game, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

4. Data Safety (Google Play)

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements:

✅ No personal data is collected or shared.
✅ No location data is collected.
✅ No user content is uploaded.
✅ High scores are stored locally only (never transmitted).
✅ In-app purchases are handled by Google Play.

5. Cookies

The Game does not use cookies. AdMob may use advertising identifiers (Android Advertising ID) which can be reset or disabled in your device settings under Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID.

6. Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to secure. Local game data (scores, preferences) is stored using Android's standard SharedPreferences and Room database mechanisms.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
