Privacy Policy · Last updated: May 2026

Summary: Blue Horizons stores your data locally on your device. We do not sell your personal information. We use anonymised analytics and crash reports to improve the app. Health Connect data never leaves your device.

1. Who We Are

Blue Horizons is developed by an independent developer team, not a medical or clinical organisation. We are not licensed therapists, physicians, or sleep specialists.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Profile data — name, age, gender (entered voluntarily during onboarding)
  • Wellness data — mood check-ins, journal entries, habit logs, sleep schedule preferences
  • Health Connect data — sleep and activity data read from Android Health Connect (only if you grant permission)
  • Analytics — anonymised app usage events via Firebase Analytics (e.g. screens visited, features used)
  • Crash reports — anonymised crash and error data via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Performance data — anonymised app performance traces via Firebase Performance Monitoring

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalise the app's features
  • To display your mood history, habit trends, and journal entries
  • To send sleep and wake reminders (only if you enable notifications)
  • To fix bugs and improve performance via anonymised crash and analytics data

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

All personal data (profile, mood logs, journal, habits) is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. It is never uploaded to our servers.

Analytics and crash data are sent to Google Firebase infrastructure using anonymised identifiers.

5. Health Connect

If you grant Health Connect permission, the app reads sleep and activity data solely to display it within your personal habit and sleep views. This data:

  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Is never used for advertising or profiling
  • Is processed entirely on-device

You can revoke Health Connect permissions at any time via Android Settings → Health Connect → App permissions.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. The only data that leaves your device is anonymised analytics and crash reports sent to Google Firebase, which are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

7. In-App Purchases

Premium features are purchased through Google Play. We do not process or store any payment information. All billing is handled by Google and governed by the Google Play Terms of Service.

8. Not a Medical Service

Important: Blue Horizons is a personal wellness companion, not a medical app. Nothing in the app constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical or mental health concerns.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Your Rights

  • Access & deletion — all your personal data is on your device; uninstalling the app deletes it
  • Health Connect — revoke permissions at any time via Android Settings
  • Analytics opt-out — you can opt out of Firebase Analytics via your device's Google account settings

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance.
