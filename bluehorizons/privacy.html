PRIVACY POLICY - BLUE HORIZONS

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

Blue Horizons is developed by an independent developer team and is not a medical or clinical organisation. We are not licensed therapists, physicians or sleep specialists.

2. Data That Stays on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers:

  • Profile — name, age and gender you enter during onboarding
  • Wellness — mood check-ins, journal entries, habit logs and sleep schedule preferences
  • Journal media — optional photos and audio notes you add to journal entries
  • Health Connect — sleep, steps, exercise, hydration and mindfulness session data, only if you grant permission
  • Screen time — daily usage stats for social apps you choose to monitor, plus installed-app checks used only to show relevant options

3. Data Sent to Google Services

We use Google services to operate and improve the app. These services may receive pseudonymous technical data such as Firebase Installation ID, screen views, feature usage counts, crash reports, performance diagnostics, Remote Config fetches, and purchase-related analytics metadata such as plan or purchase event status. These events do not include journal text, mood values, note text, Health Connect values or wellness entry content. Advertising ID collection is disabled.

4. How We Use Data

We use data to provide app features, display your own history and personal trends, send reminders you enable, troubleshoot bugs, verify Premium access and improve reliability. We do not use your personal wellness content for advertising, medical profiling or clinical decision-making.

5. Health Connect

If you grant Health Connect permission, Blue Horizons reads selected sleep, steps, exercise, hydration and mindfulness session data solely to display it in your personal habit, sleep, hydration and mindfulness views. Health Connect data is processed on-device, is not sent to our servers, is not sold, is not used for ads and is not used for profiling. You can revoke Health Connect permissions at any time in Android Settings → Health Connect → App permissions.

6. Screen-Time and Installed-App Access

If you enable digital-detox habits, Blue Horizons uses Android Usage Access to calculate daily screen time and app usage for the social apps you choose to monitor. The app may also check whether common social apps are installed so it can show useful choices. This information stays on your device and is used only for habit tracking. You can revoke Usage Access at any time in Android Settings → Apps → Special app access → Usage access.

7. Music Visualizer

The music visualizer is generated by the app using animation and playback state. It does not use the microphone, record audio, capture audio output or listen to your surroundings.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your personal wellness data. Optional journal photos and audio notes stay on your device unless you choose to share an entry through another app or service. The only data that leaves your device automatically is pseudonymous analytics, crash, performance, Remote Config and purchase-event metadata sent to Google services under Google’s policies.

9. In-App Purchases

Premium features are purchased through Google Play. Monthly and yearly Premium plans are subscriptions; lifetime Premium is a one-time non-consumable purchase. Blue Horizons does not process or store payment card information. Google Play handles payments, renewals, cancellations and refunds under Google Play terms.

10. Not a Medical Service

Blue Horizons is a personal wellness companion, not a medical app. Nothing in the app is medical advice, diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical or mental-health concerns.

11. Children’s Privacy

Blue Horizons is intended for users who are at least 13 years old, or the minimum age required in their country or region, whichever is higher. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under that age. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can delete it where applicable.

12. Your Rights

You can delete locally stored app data by editing or deleting entries, uninstalling the app or clearing app data. You can revoke Health Connect, notification and Usage Access permissions in Android Settings. You can request deletion of data associated with Google services or privacy questions by contacting support@bluehorizons.app. You can control Google analytics preferences through your device and Google account settings where available.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the latest revision.

14. Contact

Questions or requests regarding privacy: support@bluehorizons.app

隐私政策 - BLUE HORIZONS

最后更新：2026年5月

1. 关于我们

Blue Horizons 由独立团队开发，不是医疗或临床机构。我们不是持证治疗师、医生或睡眠专家。

2. 保留在设备上的数据

以下数据会本地存储在您的设备上，不会上传到我们的服务器：

  • 个人资料 — onboarding 中输入的姓名、年龄和性别
  • 健康记录 — 情绪、日记、习惯和睡眠偏好
  • 日记媒体 — 你添加到日记条目的可选照片和音频笔记
  • Health Connect — 仅在授权时读取睡眠、步数、运动、饮水和正念会话数据
  • 屏幕时间 — 所选社交应用的每日使用情况，以及仅为显示相关选项而检查已安装应用

3. 发送给 Google 服务的数据

我们使用 Google 服务来运行和改进应用。这些服务可能接收假名化技术数据，例如 Firebase Installation ID、页面浏览、功能使用次数、崩溃报告、性能诊断、Remote Config 和购买事件元数据。这些事件不包含日记文本、情绪值、笔记、Health Connect 值或健康内容。广告 ID 收集已关闭。

4. 我们如何使用数据

我们使用数据来提供应用功能、显示你的历史和个人趋势、发送你启用的提醒、排查错误、验证 Premium 访问并提高可靠性。我们不会将你的个人健康内容用于广告、医疗画像或临床决策。

5. Health Connect

如果您授权 Health Connect，Blue Horizons 只会读取选定的睡眠、步数、运动、饮水和正念会话数据，用于您的个人视图。这些数据在设备上处理，不发送到服务器，不出售，不用于广告或画像。

6. 屏幕时间和已安装应用

如果启用数字排毒习惯，Blue Horizons 使用 Android Usage Access 计算所选社交应用的屏幕时间和使用情况。应用也可能检查已安装社交应用以显示有用选项。信息保留在设备上。

7. 音乐可视化

音乐可视化由应用通过动画和播放状态生成。它不使用麦克风，不录音，不捕获音频输出，也不监听周围环境。

8. 数据共享

我们不会出售、出租或共享您的个人健康数据。可选的日记照片和音频笔记会留在设备上，除非你选择通过其他应用或服务分享某条日记。自动离开设备的只有发送到 Google 服务的假名化分析、崩溃、性能、Remote Config 和购买元数据。

Premium 功能通过 Google Play 购买。月度和年度 Premium 计划是订阅；lifetime Premium 是一次性非消耗型购买。Blue Horizons 不处理或存储支付卡信息。Google Play 根据其条款处理付款、续订、取消和退款。
